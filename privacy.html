Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Emerado Museum respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information You Provide

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include in your message (such as your email address and the content of the email). We use this information only to respond to your inquiry.

Contact Form

The contact form on this website does not store or transmit data to a server. It opens your email client with a pre-filled email message. If you prefer, you may email us directly at info@emeradomuseum.org.

Cookies & Analytics

This website is designed to work without requiring cookies. If analytics are enabled by the hosting provider, they may collect basic, aggregated usage data. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information through this website.

Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party services (for example, map links). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@emeradomuseum.org.
